Instructions

You're Drilling for Oil!



You are exploring land for oil. On each round, you can choose a spot of land to drill for oil. The amount of oil you got for drilling will be indicated by a colored square where you drilled as well as the height and color of a bar on the left side of the screen.

Other people will be playing the game at the same time as you. At time, you may be able to see where they drilled and/or how much oil they got for drilling. They might also be able to see where you drilled and how much you got. If you drill in the same spot as someone else you will receive the same amount of oil, In other words, oil is not divided by the number of people drilling in the same spot.

For each patch of land, you will have 15 rounds of drilling.

You will have 30 seconds for each round.

The game will move to the next round once every player has drilled or the time is up. If the time passes before you choose a place to drill, you will get no points for that round, which will be seen as an empty bar with a red "X" in the middle.
