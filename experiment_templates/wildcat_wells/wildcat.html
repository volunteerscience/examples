Instructions

You're Drilling for Oil!



You are exploring land for oil. On each round, you can choose a spot of land to drill for oil. At the start of the following round, you will see how much oil you obtained (and therefore how many points you earned).

The amount of oil you got for drilling will be indicated by a colored square where you drilled (cool colors are low points, hot colors are high points), as well as the height and color of a bar on the left side of the screen.

Other people will be playing the game at the same time as you. You will be able to see where they drilled and how much oil they got for drilling in those locations, and they will be able to see where you drilled and how much you got. Just mouse over the bar or the colored square to see exactly how much oil (and therefore how many points) that spot gave you. If you drill in the same spot as someone else you will receive the same amount of oil, even if it is on the same round. In other words, oil is not divided by the number of people drilling in the same spot. In fact, you can copy someone's success by clicking on their bar on the left.



For each patch of land, you will have 15 rounds of drilling.

You will have 90 seconds for the first round, and 30 seconds for all remaining rounds.

The game will move to the next round once every player has drilled or the time is up. If the time passes before you choose a place to drill, you will get no points for that round, which will be seen as an empty bar with a red "X" in the middle.
