Consent

The data for the experiment you participated in can be used in a research study to understand how groups collaborate.

  • You must be 18 to participate.
  • Your participation is voluntary and uncompensated.
  • You can stop contributing data at any time.
  • Your data will remain private and confidential.
  • Only data related to your performance will be used.
  • The possible risks of this research are minimal.

This experiment has been approved by Northeastern University (IRB #13-03-09).

If you have questions about this experiment, contact irb@volunteerscience.com.

If you have questions about your rights as a participant, contact irb@neu.edu.
